Why SALVOx?
Traditional treatments often damage healthy tissues. SALVOx uses engineered bacteria to kill tumors with precision and safety.

About the Project

SALVOx uses engineered Salmonella typhimurium wrapped in a smart nanoparticle. These bacteria release a tumor-killing toxin only when inside tumors and self-destruct when outside — ensuring both precision and safety.

Core Concept

  • Target: Tumor microenvironments (low pH, low oxygen, high ROS)
  • Action: Bacteria activate gene circuits only in tumor conditions
  • Result: Cytolysin A is released → tumor cell death
  • Safety: Bacteria self-destruct outside tumors via kill switch

Gene Circuit Design

  • Cytolysin A (ClyA): Toxin that kills tumor cells
  • pnarG + pasr: Promoters for hypoxia & acidity → AND logic gate
  • psoxS: Oxygen-sensitive promoter → activates kill switch
  • Lysis Protein E: Destroys bacteria in healthy tissue

Smart Delivery System

  • Outer Layer (Polyketal): pH-sensitive biopolymer
  • Inner Layer (ZIF-8): Protects from ROS and oxygen
  • Trigger: Breaks down only in tumor-specific environments

Safety & Regulation

  • Organism: Salmonella typhimurium (Risk Group 2)
  • Lab Level: BSL-2
  • Kill Switch: psoxS-triggered lysis gene
  • Regulation in India: IBSC, DBT, GEAC, CDSCO, ICMR
